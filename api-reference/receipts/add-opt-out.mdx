---
title: "Add receipt opt-out"
description: "Add an email address to the receipt integrations opt-out list."
api: "POST https://api.ramp.com/developer/v1/receipt-integrations/opt-out"
---

## Body

<ParamField body="email" type="string" required>
  Email address to opt out of receipt integrations.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="business_id" type="number" required>
  Business ID.
</ParamField>

## Response

Returns `204 No Content` on success.
