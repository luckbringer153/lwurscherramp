The Model Context Protocol (MCP) enables AI assistants to securely connect to your business data and systems. Instead of copying and pasting information or switching between applications, you can ask your AI assistant direct questions about your Ramp account and get instant, accurate answers.

## MCP Options

  <Card title="Ramp MCP" icon="robot" href="/tools/ramp-mcp">
    Connect Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, or other AI assistants directly to your Ramp account. Query spend data, approve transactions, manage reimbursements, and more — all in natural language.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Developer MCP" icon="code" href="/tools/developer-mcp">
    A specialized MCP server for developers building with Ramp's API. Get real-time API help, schema lookups, and implementation guidance — no Ramp account required.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
